About Us
Passionate About Perfect Hedges
Dedicated, professional, and exclusively focused on hedging since day one.

We are a dedicated, professional business that prides ourselves on providing the highest quality hedging at affordable prices. Quality, Reliability, Availability and Customer Service are our core values.

Our hedge nursery is dedicated to one thing — hedging. We grow our own stock using the revolutionary Airpot system and regularly source from Europe's finest nurseries. We have established excellent relationships with specialist growers to ensure consistent availability of the highest quality plants.

Our professionally qualified and friendly staff are always on hand to provide expert advice on all aspects of hedging — from species selection and site assessment to planting and maintenance — to suit your needs, site conditions and budget.

Our Services
Everything Your Hedge Needs
Premium sourcing, expert installation, species-specific ongoing care.
01

Supply

Hedging is available bare root, root ball and containerised depending on site, season, species and budget. The majority is supplied as root ball from mid-September to end of March. Our Airpot stock is available year-round — creating mature sections that give immediate screening and security.

  • Containerised, root ball & bare root
  • Year-round Airpot-grown stock
  • European nursery partnerships
  • Immediate screening at any height
Get Quote
02

Plant

Hedges are planted for screening, garden structure, boundaries, or wildlife. The key to successful establishment is expert planting. Planting mature hedging is a long-term investment — there are golden rules that need adhering to.

  • Professional site assessment
  • Correct depth, spacing and technique
  • Screening, structure & habitat creation
  • Long-term investment protection
Discuss Project
03

Maintain

We undertake all aspects — from topping newly planted hedges to restoring very large overgrown ones. Over-management or cutting at the wrong time can be as damaging as neglect. Our species-specific approach creates beautiful, healthy hedges with longevity.

  • Species-specific maintenance plans
  • Seasonal scheduling
  • Overgrown hedge restoration
  • Noise attenuation & habitat diversity
